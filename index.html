Tiny, All Powerful Sorcerer

    You are a super-powerful flying sorcerer, who is also very small. Your forest is being attacked, and you must rescue your small animal friends from evil.

    Genre

    Tiny, All Powerful Sorcerer would be an action game from a 2D side-view perspective.

    Gameplay

    The player will control a small sorcerer, who can run and jump, and can press any one of three different keys to cast 3 different spells. One will shoot attack in a direction to kill or stun enemies, one will propel the sorcerer so they can fly, and the last can be used to lead small animals to safety. The player will aim these abilities using the mouse, and cannot use more than one at once (so flying while attacking/leading is hard). The goal of the game is to lead all the small animals back to the meadow without dying to enemies.

    Features

    Pretty setting, cute animals, exciting action.

    Story

    You’re a sorcerer hanging with a bunch of cute animals til some folks come in and make a big ruckus.

    Target Audience

    This game is intended to reach fans of Choplifter and Luftrausers, and other 2D flying action games.

    Hardware Platforms

    Anything with keyboard + mouse that can view Javascript games I guess.

The animal-leading spell can be changed/removed if time isn’t permitting. Competitive Analysis Game will realistically be competing with other student projects, and not with a general market of games, but other contending games will mostly be free-moving action games. These games are usually known for flashy graphics and powerful user feedback, so this game would seek to achieve the industry standard in those areas.
